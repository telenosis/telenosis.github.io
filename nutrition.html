Why is nutrition important?

A healthy diet throughout life supports normal growth, development and ageing, helps to maintain a healthy body weight, and reduces the risk of chronic disease. It may be one of the most important factors contributing to overall good health and well-being.
Try to include in your diet:

  • Fruit
  • Leafy greens
  • Whole grains
  • Eggs
  • Salmon
  • Chicken breast
  • Soups



People who regularly eat:

  • Foods high in fats, free sugars, or salt
  • Not enough fruit, vegetables, and whole grains
  • Whole grains

are more likely to develop chronic diseases like diabetes, heart disease and cancer.
